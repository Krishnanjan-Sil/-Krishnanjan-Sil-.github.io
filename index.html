About Me

Hi, I’m Krishnanjan Sil, a passionate theoretical physicist dedicated to exploring the fundamental laws of the universe. My fascination with the cosmos began as a child gazing at the stars, sparking a lifelong journey into quantum mechanics, cosmology, and particle physics. I strive to bridge theoretical insights with real-world data, contributing to our understanding of the universe’s deepest mysteries. When I’m not crunching equations, you’ll find me capturing the night sky or diving into science fiction.

Research Interests

  • Quantum Mechanics: Exploring quantum entanglement, superposition, and quantum information theory.
  • Cosmology: Investigating the early universe, cosmic inflation, and dark energy.
  • Particle Physics: Studying fundamental particles and their interactions in high-energy environments.
  • General Relativity: Analyzing gravitational waves and black hole dynamics.
